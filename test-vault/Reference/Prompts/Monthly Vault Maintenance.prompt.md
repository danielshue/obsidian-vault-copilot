name: Monthly Vault Maintenance
description: Audit the vault for orphaned notes, broken links, and organizational improvements
tools: [search_vault, read_note, list_tasks]
---
Perform a monthly vault maintenance audit.

**Check for:**
1. **Orphaned notes** — Notes with no inbound links
2. **Broken links** — Wikilinks that point to non-existent notes
3. **Empty notes** — Notes with little or no content
4. **Tag consistency** — Tags that might be duplicates or misspelled

Provide a summary with recommendations for cleanup.
