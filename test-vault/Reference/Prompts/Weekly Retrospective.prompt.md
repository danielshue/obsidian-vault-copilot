---
name: Weekly Retrospective
description: Generate a weekly retrospective summarizing wins, blockers, and priorities for next week
tools: [search_vault, read_note, list_tasks]
---
Review the vault for activity from this past week and create a weekly retrospective.

**Include:**
1. **Wins** — What was accomplished this week?
2. **Blockers** — What challenges or blockers were encountered?
3. **Next Week Priorities** — What should be the focus areas for next week?

Summarize findings in a clear, actionable format.
